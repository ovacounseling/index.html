Great to meet you —
I'm Chinyere.

I'm a Licensed Clinical Social Worker with experience supporting individuals overcome anxiety, depression, low self-esteem, and relationship issues. I have years of professional experience across hospital, community mental health, school, and corporate settings.

This range shapes my approach to therapy, as I consistently opt to focus on the full context of your life — including relationships, environment, and lived experiences — rather than just symptoms.

I specialize in working with adults navigating grief and life transitions, with a particular passion for supporting women and individuals exploring identity, cultural experiences, and personal growth. I also value creating a space where clients feel fully seen and understood when they embody their full selves.

My approach is warm, collaborative, and strengths-based. I believe you already hold the capacity for growth and healing, and my role is simply to help you access it, build on it, and move forward with intentionality.

If you've been feeling overwhelmed, stuck, or just need someone to talk things through with — I'm here to support you.
